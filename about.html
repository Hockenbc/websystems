About ShowBiz

ShowBiz was created to bring the best of shows, movies, anime, and manga together in one community space. Find the recommendations that match your interests and discover new favorites while keeping track of current favorites.

Why ShowBiz? Because every good story deserves a spotlight. Whether it's a binge-worthy anime, a blockbuster movie, or a trending series, ShowBiz helps viewers stay in the loop without the clutter.
